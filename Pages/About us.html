Welcome to the Scania Trucks About Us page. Here, you can learn more about our company, our mission, and our commitment to sustainability and innovation in the commercial vehicle industry.

Our Mission

At Scania, our mission is to drive the shift towards a sustainable transport system. We aim to provide innovative solutions that enhance efficiency and reduce environmental impact.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leader in sustainable transport solutions, creating value for our customers and society as a whole.

Our History

Founded in 1891, Scania has a rich history of engineering excellence and innovation. Over the years, we have evolved from a small manufacturer of horse-drawn vehicles to a global leader in commercial vehicles.
